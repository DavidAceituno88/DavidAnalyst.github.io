The skilled trades shortage crisis

Case of study

Looking ahead, it is unlikely the issue will be resolved without more intervention from government, unions and trade associations, he says. If the current situation doesn’t improve, Canada could be facing a shortage of 100,000 tradespeople by 2029- Kieran Hawe, chief operating officer of construction services company EllisDon Corp.(1)

Well, this goes far beyond your state, province, or your country, this is a worldwide scenario now. Yes, the Skilled Trade Crisis. Some experts say that the main cause is the lack of interest from the new generations in the Trades since now you can get "easier" jobs that will be less physically demanding, others say that now everyone wants to become an Online Celebrity, and so on.

The truth is that regardless of the many reasons we can come up with, we need to have actual data that reveals the reasons behind this crisis; that's why I worked together with the Trades School of the city where I used to live (this is a 2021 analysis, I was living in Mexico at that time).

The Trades School had 4 Goals / Questions:

  • 1.- What is the median age of our students?
  • 2.- What was their motivation to join the school?
  • 3.- How did they get to know about our courses?
  • 4.- How can we be more efficient in recruiting more students?

So the school during the pandemic had an online registration website that stored all of their registered students on a Data Base and that's what they gave me.

This .csv file had all the information about the student, some key fields were the Date when they registered for the course, the name of the course, age, gender if they had a job, motive for joining the trades school, how did they find out about the courses, etc. So first I had to rename most of the fields, and join others, this is my query (a very simple one).

Now with that, i got all that i needed to create a Power Bi dashboard.

The BI Dashboard


The results

  • Median age: 31 years
  • Reasons why they joined the trades school: Self improvement, To get a Job and Improve actual job salary(position). Those where the main reasons with Self Improvement being the bigest of them all 67%.
  • How did they find out about the courses? Friends and Family, other and via Internet.

How can we be more efficient in recruiting more students?

Given the results of the analysis, we can take different paths or different approaches to this question. Since most of the people (at least in that city) knew about the school because of Friends it will be a good idea to create a strategy that involves human interaction, like giving discounts if you invite or get a friend or relative to join one of the courses, given that the person to person publicity seems to be, by far, the most efficient one.

Example

Now, the areas with the most interest and number of students are Web and Mobile Applications development, Electrician, Welding, English, and Carpentry. So let's say that we want to promote even more the Web and Mobile Applications development course by looking at the statistics we can see that the potential student will be a Female, 36 years old, unemployed who wants to improve herself. That will give the school an idea of how to create a good publicity campaign.

Or let's say that the city told the school that there is a crisis in the welding or carpentry trades, and they need to increase the number of students in those areas. Then the school should focus on Males in their late 20s or early 30s who are unemployed and want to Improve themselves or get a job.

They should also take into consideration that the month with the most number of registrations is August and that it would be a good idea if they invite students to promote the trades within their social circle.

Conclusion

This case of study might not be the same scenario of what's happening in other parts of the world, but it is, in my opinion, a great start for other countries that want to take action on this Crisis. There is a great difference between having an idea of why, and knowing why. I do believe that we, of course, need to motivate the new generations to discover a love for the trades, but we must also know how to do it in the most efficient ways so that soon we can overcome this crisis, and get that leaking ceiling fixed.
